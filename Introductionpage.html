Set Designer

Working on a set is more than just creating the setting for a story. Its the foundation of the tale being displayed to a audience. I would love to do this career in my lifetime and be able to make people's stories come to life.


Photographer
This career not only gives a person the ability to capture something beautiful but to show a different aspect of the picture taken.A photographer has to be able to embodie the whole landscape in one photo.



Fine Artist

A fine artist puts their world in canvases to make people feel their emotions or to make people see a different perspective of something. Being a artist in general is giving people insight into something they don't see or feel every day.


Art Director

A art director is a impressive job but a person has to be able to handle a load of responsibility. To choose which art piece is the best to show in the gallery. Art directors have to unify the whole unique vision of the gallary.
